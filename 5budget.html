Top 5 Budget

5 - Honor Play

The Honor Play has a 6.3 1080p display which runs on Android 8.1 Oreo. This device has 4 gigs of RAM. The current price of this phone is around $450 USD.

4 - BLU VIVO XI+

The Blu Vivo XI+ has 128 gigs of storage with 6 gigs of RAM. This smartphone has a 2 GHz Helio p60 octa-core processor. The Blu Vivo XI+ has a 6.2 inch display via Gorilla Glass 3 protection. The current price of this budget smartphone is around $250 USD.

3 - Nokia 7.1

The Nokia 7.1 has 4 gigs of RAM and 64 gigs of storage with a 1.8 GHz Qualcomm Snapdragon 636 processor. The Nokia 7.1 comes with a 5.84 inch display. The price of this budget smartphone is around $430 USD.

2 - Pocophone F1

The Pocophone F1 has an incredible 2.8 GHz Octa Core Qualcomm Snapdragon 845 processor with 6 gigs of RAM and up to 256 gigs of internal storage. This amazing budget smartphone has a 6.18 inch 1080p display. The price of the Pocophone F1 by Xiaomi is around $450 USD.

1 - Huawei P30 Lite

The Huawei P30 Lite has 6 gigs of RAM and 128 gigs of storage. It runs with the Cure on 710 chipset. The Huawei P30 Lite has a 6.5 LCD display. The price of this smartphone is around $330 USD.
