Top Budget Dewdrop Smartphones

May 14, 2019

What are the best budget dewdrop smartphones of 2019 so far? Today I will inform you of the best budget dewdrop smartphones inckuding the Nokia 4.2,Honor Play 8A, Samsung Galaxy A50, HUAWEI y7 2019, Huawei P Smart 2019,and the Xiaomi Redmi Note 7.


For the operating systems of these budget smartphones, the Honor 8A runs on Android 9.0 Pie with EMUI 9. However, the Huawei Y7 2019 only runs on Android 8.1 Oreo with EMUI 8.2. The Redmi Note 7 runs on Android 9 pie with MIUI 10. The Huawei P Smart 2019 operates with Android 9 Pie with MIUI 9. However, the Nokia 4.2 runs on Android 9 Pie but including stock Android with the Android one program. Personally, I like stock Android better than MIUI. If it has EMUI or MIUI, I would install Nova Launcher, and I’ll be good to go. The winner of the operating systems would be the Nokia 4.2 because it has the best os with stock Android 9 Pie.


For the processor of these budget smartphones, the Honor 8A has a Mediatek Helio P35. The Huawei Y7 2019 has a Qualcomm Snapdragon 450 with 40 nanometers. The Xiaomi Redmi Note 7 has a Qualcomm Snapdragon 660. Nevertheless, the Nokia 4.2 has a Snapdragon 439. The difference between these Snapdragon processors is that the 660 has 14 nanometers while the Nokia 4.2 has only 12 nanometers. However, for the Huawei P Smart 2019, the processor is a Hisilicon Kirin 710 including only 12 nanometers similar to the Nokia 4.2.


The Redmi Note 7 has 128, 64, or 32 gigs of internal storage with 4, 6, or 3 gigs of RAM. the Nokia 4.2 only has 32 or 16 gigs of internal storage with only 3 or 2 gigs of RAM. The Huawei P Smart 2019 has 64 or 32 GB of internal storage with 3 gigs of RAM. The Huawei Y7 2019 has 32 gigabytes of internal storage with 3 gigs of RAM while the Honor Play 8A has 64 or 32 gigs of internal storage with 3 gigs of RAM. The Samsung Galaxy A50 has 128 or 64 gigabytes of internal storage. The Samsung Galaxy A50 has 6 or 4 gigs of RAM. The winner of this category of internal storage and RAM would certainly be the Samsung Galaxy A50.


The prices of these incredible dewdrop smartphones are unbelievable. The demand for the Honor 8A is around $150 USD while the Huawei Y7 2019 holds a price of around $160 USD. The Huawei P Smart 2019 costs about $180 USD while the Xiaomi Redmi Note 7 has a charge of around $170 USD. The Nokia 4.2 has a price of about $190 USD as the Samsung Galaxy A50 has a price of around $290. Essentially, you should consider buying a Samsung Galaxy A30 instead of the A50 if you’re on a $200 budget.


In conclusion, any of these budget smartphones could be the budget dewdrop king, but I think the Nokia 4.2 owns the looks while the Redmi Note 7 has the speed concerning its price.




By Riley Richter
