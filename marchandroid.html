Apps to Download:

-Nova Launcher

-Nova Launcher Prime *Optional*

-KWGT

-Huk KWGT

-Minty Free Icon Pack

-Navigation Gestures

-Google Wallpapers

Directions

     First, download Nova Launcher or Nova Launcher Prime. Then, download KWGT and the Huk KWGT widget. Next, download the Moxy icon pack, Navigation Gestures, and Google Wallpapers. All of these apps will be available in the Google Play Store.

     Open Nova Launcher and set your desktop grid to 9 by 5. Disable your dock in Nova Launcher. Set your icon pack to Minty Free in Nova Launcher. Set the wallpaer in the picture to the left found under the Cityscapes category in the Google Wallpapers app. Enable gesture pill in Navigation Gestures. Set your pill width to 75px and hight to 37px. Enable the X position of the pill to 0px and the vertical position to 37px. Add the Google search widget towards the bottom of home screen. Add your favorite icons to the homescreen in a row of 5 by 3. Add the KWGT widget on the top of your homescreen. Tap on the widget and choose the Huk KWGT widget pack. Choose the widget FREE 068 and add it to your homescreen. You can enlarge the widget in the touch heading. Make sure you saved your changes to the widget. I hope you enjoy this Nova Launcher Setup!
